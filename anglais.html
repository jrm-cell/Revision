Structure & Phrases Clés

1. Salutations (Greetings)

Formel : Dear Mr/Ms Smith, / Dear Sir or Madam,
Standard : Dear John, / Hello Everyone,

2. Objet (Opening lines)

Standard : I am writing you about... / I am writing to you regarding...
Information : I am writing to inform you that...
Suite à un échange : Following our meeting... / Further to our phone call...

3. Demande & Pièces jointes

Demande : Could you please send me...? / I would be grateful if you could...
Fichiers : Please find attached the document... / I have attached the file...

4. Clôture & Politesse

Attente : I look forward to hearing from you.
Aide : If you have any questions, please feel free to contact me.
Signature : Best regards, / Kind regards,

Exemple Professionnel

Subject: Invitation to the Workshop

Dear Delphine,

I hope this email finds you well.

I am writing you about the cybersecurity workshop that will take place in our office next Thursday at 2 PM.

It would be a pleasure to have you among us. Please let me know if you are available to attend.

I look forward to hearing from you soon.

Best regards,
(La tu signe en fonction de ton RP , Jimmyyyyyyyyyyyyy)

Points de vigilance

🚫 AVIS PERSONNEL : Il est strictement interdit de donner son avis personnel ("I think", "In my opinion") SAUF si la consigne le demande explicitement.
Nombre de mots : Respecte scrupuleusement la consigne (ex: 150 mots). Note le total à la fin.
Majuscules : Jours (Monday), Mois (April), Nationalités et "I" (je) prennent TOUJOURS une majuscule.
↔Mise en page : Saute des lignes entre l'objet, la salutation, les paragraphes et la signature finale.
